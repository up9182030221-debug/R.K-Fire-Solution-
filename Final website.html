What we do

Refilling, recharging, and inspection — under one roof.

Whether it's a single home cylinder or a full office stock, we handle the complete cycle so your extinguishers stay compliant and ready.

SERVICE 01

Cylinder Refilling

Refilling for ABC dry powder and CO2 cylinders of all sizes, with correct agent weight and pressure as per standard.

SERVICE 02

New Connection / Installation

New extinguisher cylinder setup for homes, shops, offices, and vehicles — including foam cylinders and automatic ABC fire balls (500g) for quick self-activating protection.

SERVICE 03

Inspection & Maintenance

Pressure checks, seal and hose inspection, and tagging so you always know when the next service is due.

SERVICE 04

Hydro Testing

Periodic hydrostatic testing of cylinders to confirm they're safe to refill and continue in service.

SERVICE 05

AMC / Bulk Servicing

Annual maintenance contracts for offices, factories, and societies — scheduled visits, no follow-up calls needed.

SERVICE 06

On-site Pickup & Drop

We can collect your cylinder for refilling and return it fully serviced — minimal disruption to you.
